About StyleNest

We believe fashion should be accessible, sustainable, and deeply personal. Since 2015, we've been dressing India's modern generation with curated style at honest prices.

Our Story
Born from a Passion for Style

StyleNest was founded in 2015 by Rohan Kapoor and Meera Nair, two fashion enthusiasts who were frustrated by the gap between high-fashion aesthetics and affordable pricing in India.


Starting with just 200 SKUs in a small Mumbai office, we've grown to over 5,000 styles, 120+ partner brands, and deliveries across 28 states. Our mission remains the same: bring curated, quality fashion to every Indian home.


Today, StyleNest is trusted by over 8 lakh customers and processes 12,000+ orders every month. We work directly with weavers, manufacturers, and independent designers to ensure fair pricing and ethical sourcing.

Help
Frequently Asked Questions
What sizes do you offer?
StyleNest offers sizes from XS to 3XL for most categories. Our size chart is available on every product page. We also offer Plus Size collections with dedicated fits for sizes 2XL and above. If you're between sizes, our chatbot can help you find the right fit based on your measurements.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 4-7 business days. Express delivery (1-3 days) is available at ₹199. Same-day delivery is available in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, and Hyderabad. Orders placed before 2 PM are dispatched the same day.
What is your return policy?
We offer 30-day hassle-free returns on most products. Items should be unused, unwashed, and with original tags. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Free returns are available via pickup in 200+ cities. You can initiate a return through the website, app, or WhatsApp.
Are the product images accurate?
We use professional photography with accurate color representation. However, slight variations may occur due to screen calibration. All product descriptions include exact fabric composition, measurements, and care instructions. We have a 100% return policy if the product doesn't match the description.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery?
Yes, COD is available across all 28,000+ pincodes we serve. A ₹50 handling charge applies to COD orders. We accept all major UPI apps, credit/debit cards, net banking, and EMI options with 0% interest on orders above ₹2,999.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track orders through your StyleNest account dashboard or by contacting our support team at hello@stylenest.in or calling 1800-103-7799.
Do you have a loyalty or membership programme?
Yes! StyleNest Gold membership is available at ₹299/year. Benefits include free express delivery, early access to sales, exclusive member discounts (extra 10% on all orders), birthday rewards, and priority customer support.
Are your products genuine and quality-checked?
Absolutely. Every product on StyleNest goes through a 12-point quality check before dispatch. We work directly with verified manufacturers and certified brands. StyleNest Originals are our in-house label, manufactured under strict quality guidelines. All partner brands like Arrow, Manyavar, and Libas are officially authorised sellers.
